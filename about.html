About Buffer Bros

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by two friends with a passion for cars and perfection, Buffer Bros started as a small mobile detailing service with just a hose and a dream.

What began as a side hustle quickly grew into a thriving business as word spread about our meticulous attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we operate a fully-equipped mobile detailing rig serving the entire Marco / Naples area, but we've never lost sight of the personal touch that made us successful in the first place.

Our Philosophy

At Buffer Bros, we believe that detailing isn't just about cleaning a car—it's about restoring pride of ownership and preserving your vehicle's value.

We approach every vehicle with the same care we would give our own, using only premium products and techniques that deliver exceptional results without compromising your car's finish.

Our team undergoes continuous training to stay at the forefront of detailing technology and techniques, ensuring your vehicle receives the best possible care.

Our Commitment to Excellence
